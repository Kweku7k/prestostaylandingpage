✅ Presto Stay Hostel Audit Checklist

Complete this checklist monthly to prevent fraud and ensure your hostel is running efficiently.

1. Tenant Record Verification

🟢 Why this matters: Ensures transparency and eliminates "ghost tenants" — a common theft tactic by dishonest managers.

2. Room Occupancy and Allocation

🟢 Benefit: Stops unauthorized subletting and hidden income from vacant rooms.

3. Payment Reconciliation

🧾 Why this matters: Prevents managers from accepting "off-the-book" payments.

4. Maintenance & Damage Logs

🛠 Result: Encourages accountability and avoids repeated tenant complaints being ignored.

5. Security Protocols

🔒 Security metrics = Tenant trust. Boosts your selling point of "safe living."

6. Inventory Audit

📦 Why it matters: Prevents "item recycling" (taking items from one room to cover for another).

7. Manager Performance Report

📊 Transparency in performance = fewer thefts. Lets owners hold managers accountable.

8. Legal & Policy Compliance

⚖️ Standardized rules reduce disputes and legal loopholes.
